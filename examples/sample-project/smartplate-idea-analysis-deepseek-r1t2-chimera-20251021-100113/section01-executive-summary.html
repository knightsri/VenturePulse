One-Line Summary

SmartPlate is an AI-powered meal planning assistant that helps busy professionals and families save 5+ hours weekly while reducing food waste by 40-50% through intelligent ingredient optimization and personalized nutrition planning.

This creates a $12B global opportunity in meal planning software, with 73% of consumers seeking personalized nutrition advice yet finding existing solutions inadequate for managing dietary restrictions and minimizing waste.
