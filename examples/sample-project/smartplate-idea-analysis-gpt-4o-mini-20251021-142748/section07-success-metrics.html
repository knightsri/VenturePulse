Risk Register

Risk Name Category Probability Impact Description Mitigation Strategies
User Engagement/Retention Operational Medium High Low engagement may lead to high churn rates, impacting growth and revenue. Busy users may not utilize the app consistently without ongoing motivation. Implement personalized weekly emails, push notifications for meal prep reminders, and gamification elements to enhance user experience and retention.
Recipe Quality/Variety Technical Medium High Inconsistent recipe quality or lack of variety may frustrate users, leading to negative reviews and attrition. Partner with food bloggers for diverse content and establish a user-generated recipe submission process. Implement a human review system for AI-generated recipes.
Competition from Established Players Market High Medium Intense competition from established meal kit and nutrition apps could limit market share and user acquisition. Focus on unique value propositions such as AI personalization and sustainability. Build brand loyalty through customer engagement and feedback.
AI Hallucinations (Bad Recipes) Technical Medium High AI-generated recipes may occasionally contain inaccuracies, leading to user dissatisfaction and trust issues. Implement a validation layer to filter out problematic recipes, establish a user rating system, and obtain general liability insurance to cover potential issues.
