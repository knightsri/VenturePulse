AI Hallucinations / Bad Recipes Technical/Product Medium High

Description: While GPT-4 is advanced, it can still generate nonsensical or unappetizing recipes, or suggest unsafe ingredient combinations. This could lead to a poor user experience, negative reviews, and loss of trust in the AI's capabilities, undermining the core value proposition.

Mitigation: Implement a strict validation layer for AI-generated recipes, cross-referencing with a curated database for common sense checks (e.g., banned ingredient combinations, realistic cooking times). Prioritize adapting existing, validated recipes from Spoonacular over pure AI generation. Introduce a user rating and reporting system for recipes, with human review for low-rated or flagged items. Begin with human oversight for a significant portion of initial AI outputs, gradually scaling back with confidence. Consider general liability insurance.
