üìä Pricing Psychology & Strategy

  • Annual discount: Improves cash flow and reduces churn (annual retention ~75% vs. 60% monthly)
  • Free tier strategy: Generous enough to demonstrate value, limited enough to drive upgrades (1 plan/week = decision friction every Sunday)
